Jennifer Garner Seen Deeply Hugging Her Private Partner of 5 Years: 'She Deserves to Find Love Again'

By Dorcus Osongo
Jul 21, 2023
03:15 P.M.
  • Jennifer Garner and her boyfriend, John Miller, were spotted on a rare outing looking so in love with each other.
  • The actress and Miller were first linked romantically in 2018 and have dated on and off since then.
  • Garner's ex-husband, Ben Affleck, and Miller were spotted having a friendly chat.

Jennifer Garner and her boyfriend, John Miller, have been dating for five years, on and off, since 2018. The two have kept their relationship private and are rarely seen together in public.

On June 15, 2023, the couple was spotted on a rare outing while on a coffee run in Los Angeles. The two looked so in love with each other, were all smiles, and shared a tight embrace.

The "Elektra" star was dressed in navy blue leggings and a white tank top, while Miller was also in a casual look. He donned a navy-blue T-shirt and jeans. After grabbing a coffee, Garner hugged Miller goodbye. She wrapped her hands around his neck and pulled him for a close hug.

How Did Jennifer Find Love with John Out of Public Eyes?

Garner and Miller first connected in 2018 before the actress finalized her divorce from Ben Affleck, the father of her three kids. Miller has also been previously married as he and violinist Caroline Campbell divorced in 2011. Campbell and Miller share two kids.

Miller is the CEO of CaliGroup, a holding company that invests in tech and owns the CaliBurger restaurant chain. When Garner and Miller started dating in 2018, a source said what they had was casual and that Miller hadn't met the actress's kids.

Affleck and Miller shook hands, smiled at each other, and chatted for several moments before Garner joined them. The trio then got into the actress's house.

The couple reportedly split in 2020, just before the Covid-19 pandemic began, and got back together in May 2021. In 2021, the pair were photographed together for the first time since reports broke out that the two were now together again. The two were seen in New York City on a casual outing.

Since they were spotted in 2021, the two have been making rare appearances, as they were only spotted together twice in 2022. In January 2023, the two were seen holding hands while on a leisurely stroll in Santa Barbara.
